# QoreChain Academic Research Paper

## Overview

This repository contains an ongoing academic research project focused on the technological foundations, architecture, and long-term implications of QoreChain.

The objective is to analyze and document the potential role of post-quantum cryptography, artificial intelligence, and multi-VM blockchain infrastructure within next-generation decentralized ecosystems.

## Research Areas

* Post-Quantum Cryptography
* AI-Native Infrastructure
* Multi-VM Architecture
* Tokenomics Analysis
* Governance Systems
* Ecosystem Development
* Long-Term Blockchain Sustainability
